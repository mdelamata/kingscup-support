✨ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?
Tap "Start Game" from the home screen, enter the player names, and you're ready to draw cards!
Can I customize the rules?
Yes! Go to Settings → Edit Rules to change what happens for each card. You can tailor the game to your group's preferences.
What does the 4th King mean?
In the standard rules, the player who draws the 4th King must drink the "King's Cup," and the game ends.
Is the app free to play?
Yes, the app is free to download and play. We use unobtrusive ads to support development and keep the app free for everyone.

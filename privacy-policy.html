Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

🛡️ Privacy First Approach

  • Local Storage: Your game rules and settings stay on your device. We have no servers.
  • Transparent Advertising: We use Google AdMob to support the app.
  • No User Tracking: Beyond what's required for basic ad functionality, we don't track you.

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the iOS application The King's Cup (the "Application").

1. Information Handling

The Application is designed to be completely serverless. All game-related data, including custom rules, player names, and game history, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any external servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide relevant ads and measure performance, Google may collect certain data, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA or Android Ad ID).
  • Approximate location (based on IP address).
  • Ad interaction data.

This data is handled by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

3. Data Security

Since your data remains on your device, its security is protected by the standard security measures of your Operating System (iOS). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

4. Third-Party Services

Except for Google AdMob, the Application does not integrate any third-party analytics or data sharing services. We do not sell or trade any user information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage vous to review this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Application signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support channels provided in the Application or on our support website.
